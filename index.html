Mission Statement:
The Fred J. and Ruth Heflin Cofer Foundation Trust is an IRS 501 (c)(3) tax-exempt organization which exists to provide assistance to handicapped or otherwise needy individuals whose needs are unmet through public or private means, thereby following the example of Jesus Christ and as practiced by its founder Ruth Heflin Cofer. Based on demonstrated need, individual needs may be met in the form of equipment and/or services directly by the foundation as is practicable and always in the spirit of Christian principle.
